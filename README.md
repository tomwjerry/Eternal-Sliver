# Eternal Sliver

Eternal Sliver is a mobile-first 3D action RPG game that immerses players in a fantastical world filled with adventure, combat, and exploration. This document outlines the features, controls, gameplay mechanics, and future roadmap of the game.

## Features
- **Immersive 3D Environments**: Explore beautifully crafted landscapes and dungeons.
- **Dynamic Combat System**: Engage in fast-paced battles with a variety of weapons and skills.
- **Character Customization**: Personalize your hero with unique abilities, gear, and aesthetics.
- **Multiplayer Mode**: Team up with friends or challenge other players in real-time combat.
- **Quests and Storylines**: Immerse yourself in rich narratives and exciting quests that unfold as you progress.

## Controls
- **Movement**: Use the virtual joystick on the left to move your character.
- **Attack**: Tap the action button on the right to attack enemies.
- **Skill Activation**: Use designated skill buttons to perform special abilities.
- **Inventory Management**: Access your inventory through the menu button to equip items and manage resources.
- **Pause**: Tap the pause button to access settings and other options.

## Gameplay Mechanics
1. **Exploration**: Navigate through an expansive world filled with hidden treasures, enemies, and secret locations.
2. **Combat**: Engage in real-time battles against NPCs and other players, utilizing a combination of normal attacks and special skills.
3. **Leveling and Progression**: Gain experience points from battles and quests to level up your character, unlocking new abilities and equipment.
4. **Resource Management**: Gather resources to craft items, upgrade weapons, and improve your character's skills.

## Future Roadmap
- **Feature Expansion**: Introduce new quests, character classes, and multiplayer events.
- **Seasonal Events**: Implement limited-time events that offer unique challenges and rewards.
- **User Feedback Integration**: Actively collect and incorporate player feedback to enhance gameplay and user experience.
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Explore options for multi-device support, allowing players to enjoy the game on various platforms.
